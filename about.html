/ About Kolvren

We've Seen What Happens When Copilot Doesn't Stick.

Kolvren was built by practitioners who lived inside enterprise M365 deployments — and watched millions in licences underperform because organisations treated adoption as an IT problem instead of a business one.

/ Our Position

Not a Training Company. Not a Staffing Firm. A Copilot ROI Partner.

Kolvren occupies a specific gap in the market: between the Microsoft partner who sells you licences and the learning platform that sells you video courses. Neither of them is accountable for whether Copilot actually changes how your team works. We are.

We are a small, senior team of M365 practitioners, change management specialists, and B2B enablement strategists. We take a limited number of engagements per quarter — deliberately — because the quality of our work depends on being genuinely embedded with each client, not running a factory.

Our benchmark is simple: does Copilot measurably improve the work your team does? If it does, we've done our job. If it doesn't, we haven't — and we work until it does.

/ What We Stand For

Four Principles. Non-Negotiable.

/

Outcomes Over Activity

We don't measure success by decks delivered or sessions run. We measure it by adoption rate, time saved, and business impact. If the numbers don't move, we're not done.

/

Honesty Over Salesmanship

If the audit shows Copilot isn't the right priority for your organisation right now, we'll say so. Our reputation depends on giving you the right advice, not the profitable one.

/

Embedded Over Advising

We work alongside your team, inside your tools, on your real workflows. Not from a distance, not from a slide deck.

/

Senior Delivery

Every engagement is led by a senior practitioner. We don't bait-and-switch with junior consultants after the proposal is signed.
