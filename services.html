/ 02 — Core Service

Workflow Implementation

The single biggest reason Copilot adoption fails is that organisations train people on the tool instead of integrating it into the work.

Kolvren takes a workflow-first approach: we identify the processes that consume the most time in your organisation, engineer Copilot into each step, build the prompts and templates your team needs, and measure the output delta before and after.

/ Common Questions

Questions We Hear Often.

We offer the audit at no cost because it's the right way to start a relationship. It lets us understand your environment and demonstrate value before you commit to anything. Roughly 60% of audit clients proceed to an engagement. The other 40% leave with a roadmap they can act on themselves. Either outcome is fine with us.
One-time training produces a temporary lift — typically 2–3 weeks — then usage declines. Sustainable adoption requires workflow integration (so Copilot is embedded in daily tasks), role-specific prompting (so people know exactly what to ask), and a governance cadence (so adoption is measured and managed over time). Training is one input. We deliver the full system.
Our sweet spot is 50–500 employees with active Microsoft 365 Copilot licences. Below 50, our structured programmes can feel heavy. Above 500, requirements often need enterprise-scale deployment resources beyond our current team. We'll tell you honestly if we're not the right fit.
Most clients see measurable adoption movement within 4–6 weeks of workflow implementation. Quantified ROI — time saved, output quality, adoption rate hitting a target — typically lands in the 10–14 week window. We set clear benchmarks upfront and report against them monthly.
We're industry-agnostic but have deep experience in professional services, healthcare administration, financial services, logistics, and B2B technology companies. The Copilot workflows that matter most vary by industry — which is exactly why we start with an audit rather than a template.
Workflow implementation engagements start at $8,500 for a focused single-workflow build. Full adoption programmes for 50–200 seat organisations typically range from $18,000–$45,000 depending on scope. The ROI Audit is always free. We share typical investment ranges on the first call — no mystery pricing.
